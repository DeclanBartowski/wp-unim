THE DATA WE HAVE ACCUMULATED AND SUGGEST TO USE FOR ANALYSIS

We offer:

Images: 500 000
Among them DICOM: 870
Among them WSI: 150 000
Total number of cases: 100 000

In each case, statistics are kept:

  • included clinical diagnosis
  • time of incoming material registration
  • types of incoming material
  • time of passing each stage and creation of materials in the laboratory: histo slides, histoblocks, staining, creation of digital images, data availability for the pathologists
  • pathologist’s appointment time in case
  • time of adding a second pathologists into the case
  • statistics of communication between pathologists, num-ber of messages, time of registration of each message
  • statistics of additional pathologists connection
  • statistics of IHC panel assignment, slide creation, staining, scanning, uploading the case as WSI set
  • time of writing the report.
  • time of validation of the report by another pathologist
  • time of sending the report
  • links to the same patient’s cases
  • Anamnesis are contained in the system for each patient in a general unstructured format in Russian. Part of the data is structured.
    The reports are structured, in Russian.

INFO ABOUT UNIM AND DATA

UNIM Company was founded in 2014. The same year it developed its own technology for working with large images and conducting a consultation. In 2018, to improve development of the technology, the company opened its own pathomorphological laboratory in the innovation center Skolkovo (Moscow), working entirely in digital format. n 2020, the company opened one more laboratory, a full copy of the current lab. In 2020, a new contract was signed to deploy the Platform in the Khabarovsk region of Russia (digitalization of all pathology in the region).

As of autumn 2020, the company:

  • Provides diagnosis for 150-250 patients daily (5000 per month on average), the percentage of cancer cases - 10%
  • In each complicated/oncological/operational case/second opinion, at least 2 pathologists are involved in the diagnosis (average number of doctors per case is 2.12)
  • The laboratory works 7/365 and provides the following SLAs: for 80% of cases the diagnosis time is less than 72 hours for biopsy, 96 hours for the sigery one, in case of IHC the time is extended by no more than 48 hours.
  • There are 38 pathologists in the company for daily routine and not more than 2 of them are directly present in the laboratory physically.
  • There are more than 1500 active accounts from 35 countries are connected to DP system
  • The area of the laboratory - 200 square meters

The laboratory uses the following equipment:

  • Leica Aperio AT2 Histology Scanner - 2 pcs.
  • histological stainer with the module for the cover film - 1 pcs.
  • rotational microtoms - 3 pcs.
  • cassette printer - 1 pcs.
  • cutting station - 2 pcs
  • histology processor - 2 pcs.
  • Leica BOND IHC Steiner - 2 pcs.
  • average utilization rate of equipment - 80%

The laboratory uses a self-developed platform UNIM DIgital Pathology - DP, this platform includes seamless integration of three interrelated functions:

  • Lab Information System: material tracking, integration with patient’s information systems (MIS), macro description standards.
  • Digital Pathology: Consilium, assistants, communications environment, microdescription standards.
  • Statistics and control module: performance of equipment, staff, doctors, timing control, document distribution.
